Who We Are

Triple S Systems Limited was established on the 1st of April 2022 poised to be one of the leading wholesales suppliers of all kinds of 100% genuine technology products. The company provides a wide range of products in computing, enterprise, print and consumables and more from prominent brands like Hewlett- Packard, Dell Technologies, Samsung, Cisco and Microsoft. We are established on over Sixteen (16) years experience in ICT business development and sales in delivering affordable, efficient and quality products and services

We understand that our reputation and success are strongly influenced by the quality of customer relations/satisfaction at every point, thus our values are founded on great customer centricity evident in our many connections maintained over decades before our emergence. We are located in one of the main business areas in Lagos boasting direct partnerships with major brand vendors in the delivery top quality branded products.
